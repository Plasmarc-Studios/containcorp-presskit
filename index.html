Pitch

Containcorp is an Anomalous Management Sim, where you contain & exploit anomalous entities for tremendous profit, all in the name of a Corporate behemoth. Construct your facility across multiple floors, design containment areas and employ brilliant minds to study anomalies, all with the goal of maximising profit and revenue for your facility.

Contain, Research, and Profit!

Description

In Containcorp players are tasked with containing anomalies which are varied creatures and phenomena with unpredictable behaviour. Researching them unlocks technological advancements such as new weapons, items and production processes, which can feed back into your facility's pipeline to further expand its operations. The singular goal is to generate ludicrous profit off anomalies by exploiting their behaviours, at any cost.

Gameplay

Start by selecting the perfect location for your facility, and use your grant to construct containment areas, staff quarters, and research labs. Expand upwards into the sky or deep below the earth. As you progress, you'll receive requests to contain increasingly challenging anomalies, and must make strategic decisions to invest in specialised teams and equipment to successfully contain them. But it's not just about the containment, you'll also need to manage your staff and keep them sane while dealing with eldritch horrors. Be prepared to face off against hostile groups, who will stop at nothing to raid your facility and steal your anomalies for their own benefit. With a unique blend of base-building, management and anomalous containment, this game will challenge your strategic and tactical skills.

Features

  • Implement containment procedures to handle dangerous anomalies and restore order from containment breaches.
  • Engage in tactical combat with hostile entities, and hold the defence against raids.
  • Design and construct multi-story buildings or burrow underground utilising a plethora of resources.
  • Create efficient power and pipe networks grids to handle your facility's logistic needs.
  • Manage individual character injuries, desires, and emotions.
  • Conduct scientific experiments to unlock technologies with an extensive research system.
  • Onboard human test-subjects for high-risk experiments.

    • Plus many more systems.

History

Plasmarc Studios started as a duo of passionate programmers committed to crafting captivating games. We founded the studio on August 26th, 2020, as a complement to our main game Containcorp. We had no idea what we were doing at the beginning, but 5 years later, and a small team behind us, we can finally get Containcorp out the door.
