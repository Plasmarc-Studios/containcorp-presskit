Pitch

Containcorp is an Anomalous Management Sim, where you contain & exploit anomalous entities for tremendous profit, all in the name of a Corporate behemoth. Construct your facility across multiple floors, design containment areas and employ brilliant minds to study anomalies, all with the goal of maximising profit and revenue for your facility.

Contain anomalies, Research technologies, and Profit billons!

Description

In Containcorp, players assume the role of a facility director, working for a global corporation leading a new-world innovation, with the job to manage a secret facility that contains anomalies - creatures and phenomena with unpredictable behaviour.

Researching them unlocks technological advancements such as new weapons, items and production processes, which can feed back into your facility's pipeline to further expand its operations and drive profits. The singular goal is to generate ludicrous profit off anomalies by exploiting their behaviours, at any cost.

Facilities ultimately exist as "factories" that exploit anomalies to create exportable products that can be sold.

Containcorp is set in its own mythos, a culturally stagnant 1950s corporate-fueled post-apocalyse, with anomalies being continually added through development. Containcorp aims to have around 100 anomalies by 1.0.

Gameplay

Contain

Start by selecting the perfect location for your facility, and use initial contracts gracefully provided by the Corporation to construct containment areas, staff quarters, and research labs. Expand upwards into the sky or deep below the earth on multiple levels as you build out your facility. As you progress, you'll receive requests to contain challenging anomalies, and make architectual and logistical decisions to build successful containment cells. Each anomaly under you will grant you a continual bonus. A simple way to make money.

Research

Anomalies present a bible of knowledge and untapped potential. Using the best researchers you can hire and the best science labs you can build, you'll be researching new technologies, processes, products and solutions to ease the burden of containment, unlock new avenues of profit, or discover useful tech. Interns will be the backbone of your scientific method, acting as guinea pigs for experiments. But you'll have to manage your staff and keep them sane while dealing with the many horrors you'll encounter.

Profit

Utilising the unique behaviours of anomalies, you'll find ways to exploit them into lucrative logistical pipelines. To design creative machines of profit, you'll have to strike a balance between keeping anomalies safely contained and milking them for everything they have. Every anomaly is different, so there is no one size fits all. But every success comes with a cost. Be prepared to face off against hostile invading rival corporations, who will stop at nothing to disrupt your operations and put an end to your good quarter.

Features

  • Multi-story buildings (multiple z-levels).
  • Rooms, regions & Zones.
  • Electrical and plumbing networks.
  • NPCs (Nuggets) with bodyparts, injuries, desires, thoughts, emotions, schedules, and career aspirations.
  • Day shift and night shifts preferences for staff.
  • Contracts to accept and complete.
  • Financial Management via accounts and Saving.
  • Structural Integrity (Collapsable buildings).
  • Combat, Weapons, Ordnance, death and destruction.
  • Containment procedure rule designer for keeping staff safe.
  • Anomaly Experiments with Interns and guinea pigs.
  • Research tree for each anomaly, to unlock new technologies.
  • Facility Invasions by rival corporations.
  • Fires, Flooding, Radiation and Gases.
  • Multidue of staff jobs, from Cooks to Guards, Researchers to Janitors.
  • Intelligent narrative system that spawns "incidents" to keep thins interesting.
  • Anomlies Galore

    • Plus many more systems, and anything we add in the future.

History

Plasmarc Studios started as a duo of passionate programmers committed to crafting captivating games. We founded the studio on August 26th, 2020, as a complement to our main game Containcorp. We had no idea what we were doing at the beginning, but after 5 years, a million rewrites and poor design choices later, remaking the game from scratch to fix those mistakes, overhauling the art style and finding the game's "soul" along the way, and many personal revelations meanwhile; all with super small team behind us, we can finally get Containcorp out the door.
